EV-Specific Risks in the Starting Phase
⚡
Range vs the 2-Shift Model
The core risk. Real range (150–220 km) may not cover 200+ km/day across a day + night shift without a midday top-up — and a charging car earns ₹0. Fix: pick the long-range (315 km ARAI) variant, fast-charge during the shift handover gap, or run a single long shift instead of two.
🔌
Charging Infrastructure
Depends on a reliable charger + stable power; public DC fast chargers in Delhi are limited and often queued. Fix: install a dedicated 7.4 kW home/depot charger at a partner's parking — don't depend on public chargers for daily uptime.
💰
Higher Upfront → Bigger Loan
₹3–4 L more on-road means a larger EMI and more partner capital up front. Fix: the Delhi EV subsidy + road-tax/registration waiver and the ₹9,000/mo running-cost saving offset most of it over the loan term.
🔋
Battery Degradation
Capacity fades with heavy daily use; a full replacement is ₹3–5 L. Fix: the 8-year / 1.6 lakh km battery warranty covers the entire loan period — keep the emergency buffer fund for the post-warranty years.
Start With CNG If…
You have no dedicated parking/charging, want maximum uptime on two shifts from Day 1, and want the lowest-risk launch. CNG refuels in 5 minutes anywhere — the car almost never sits idle.
Go EV Now If…
A partner can install a home/depot charger and you run mostly a single long shift or predictable km. You get the lowest running cost, Delhi policy perks, and the cleanest long-term passive asset — ideal for the rent-to-driver phase later.
* Subsidy subject to the prevailing Delhi EV Policy and scheme funds at time of purchase.   ** Battery covered under standard 8-yr / 1,60,000 km warranty; figures are estimates for planning — confirm live prices and policy with the dealer before deciding.

Risk Management

Known Risks &
How We Handle Them

Every business has risks. We've identified the key ones and have clear mitigation strategies.

👷
Driver Reliability
Always maintain 1 backup driver contact. Pay on-time, treat drivers well — retention is everything. Use Ola/Uber ratings as accountability tool.
🚗
Car Accident / Breakdown
Commercial insurance covers damage. Buffer fund covers downtime cost. Maruti has 500+ service centers across Delhi for quick repairs.
📱
Ola/Uber Commission Cuts
Register on 3 platforms simultaneously. Gradually build direct corporate/hotel clients to reduce app dependency and improve margins.
🤝
Partner Disputes
Signed partnership deed clearly defines profit split, roles, exit terms. Monthly transparent P&L shared with all 5 partners via WhatsApp group.
